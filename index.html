About Shotmetrx

The Shotmetrx is an app for basketball players, trainers, coaches, and parents who want to track a players shooting performance and their made percentage. Shotmetrx lets you record every shot you attempt and make on the court, view detailed statistics, and compare your results with others. Shotmetrx has an easy to use Shot Missed Toggle (SMT) tool that tracks how players miss the shot around the rim on every missed shot. Track helpful metrics in two different modes; practice mode or game mode, and different types of shots, such as jumpshots, layups, and free throws.
